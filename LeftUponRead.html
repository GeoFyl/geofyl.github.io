Left Upon Read

Left Upon Read is medieval action/adventure rpg with a modern twist where you play as a knight battling through a dungeon while also dealing with text messages. The game was created for a group project with a multidisciplinary team, to simulate working in a professional studio environment. We recieved support from industry professionals at one of the UK's largest AAA studios. The game has been nominated by Abertay University for the Best Student Game category of the TIGA UK Games Education Awards, is being actively developed through DARE Academy and will be showcased at the Glasgow Independant Games Festival and EGX London!
