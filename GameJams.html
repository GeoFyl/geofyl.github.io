Game Jams

I have taken part in several game jams with the Abertay Games Development Society.

INPATIENT - AGDS Halloween Jam 2023

Theme: "Give"
In this psychological horror you must carry out tasks prescribed by the facility through a screen, but they slowly become more twisted...
I am quite proud of this, considering we had just 2 days and a small team.

WarSite - AGDS April Fools Jam 2022

Theme: "Make a bad game"
Following the theme, we made the worst game we could. By design, it is a buggy mess with poorly written code. Crashes when you die. It was a lot of fun!

Time Ghost Body Bag - AGDS Halloween Jam 2021

Theme: "Backwards"
You have been killed and dissected by an evil scientist! As a ghost, control time and rebuild your body while avoiding other test subjects...

We Don't Know - AGDS Halloween Jam 2022
